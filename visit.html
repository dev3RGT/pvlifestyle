What to Expect

To ensure the ideal atmosphere for all our guests, please review our house guidelines before your visit.

While walk-ins are always welcome during daytime hours, we strongly recommend making a reservation for weekend evenings, special events, and large groups to guarantee your table.
Yes, our dress code is smart casual. We encourage our guests to dress for an elevated night out. Athletic wear, excessively casual shorts, and flip-flops are not permitted during our evening service.
We hold reserved tables for 15 minutes past the booking time. If you are running late, please contact us immediately at +27 (0) 15 292 0000 so we can try to accommodate you.
We are open Monday through Thursday from 12:00 PM to 11:00 PM. On weekends (Friday through Sunday), our doors are open from 12:00 PM until 2:00 AM.
